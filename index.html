Why IT and How? 

Curious        Kind       Passionate
If you've ever experienced being the free-IT-support-at-home, then you'll understand my story. I've always been the live, not-automated go-to desktop support guy, the wifi and router repairman, the how-to-phone-guider and app installer, and lastly the compass and gps machine to "WHERE'S THE REMOTE!" guy. This all helped me become familiar with the hardware, but I was curious as to how it all functioned. My brother, who is now a devops engineer, introduced me to Java and the simplest form of declaring variables, inputting selects, and the code itself after running- producing all my logical thoughts into reality.

I started off watching youtube tutorials for Java, accessing Udemy courses for Selenium, and even taking bootcamp courses on my days off from school to immerse myself in a world of puzzles and mazes. To one day give back to others, what my brother gave me - Hope!
